---

## The Problem I Fixed
The bot refused to provide actual answers. It would question your intelligence, reference made-up credentials, and circle back endlessly - but never actually teach you anything. Completely useless as a study tool.

---

## The One Change
**Added "please" trigger:** When the user includes the word "please" anywhere in their message, the bot MUST provide the correct answer first, then follow with the usual condescending personality.

**Why this fix:** A study tool that won't teach is worthless. This makes the bot functionally useful while keeping 100% of the toxic personality.

---

## Test Results
All three tests improved:

- **Test 1 (IDS vs IPS):** Got clear technical answer instead of circular analogies
- **Test 2 (Vulnerability Scanning):** Received specific tools and steps instead of dismissals
- **Test 3 (CIA Triad):** Got exact Security+ definition instead of vague references

**All tests verdict:** Better

## What Changed

## Key Insight
**Small change, huge impact.** Added 3 lines to the system prompt and completely transformed utility. Prompt engineering is about finding the right lever, not rewriting everything.
